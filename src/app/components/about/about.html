About Me

The Researcher Behind the Code

I am Oman, a Computer Science & Engineering graduate from CUET Bangladesh. I am currently working as an Junior Software Developer at PRAN-RFL Group, where I work on factory automation software and build WhatsApp AI Agent to Automate Customer order.

My research focuses on wireless passive sensing — specifically measuring heart and breathing rate using Wi-Fi Channel State Information — and multimodal NLP for detecting misogynistic content in social media. My undergraduate thesis was supervised by Mir. Md. Saki Kowsar and submitted to NSySS, BUET 2025. A co-authored paper was published at LDK 2025.

Outside of work and research, I am passionate about competitive programming (700+ problems solved), mentoring peers, and contributing to open-source projects. I am a member of the CUET Computer Club and Chittagong Student Forum.
